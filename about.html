Our Story

AABT GROUP has been a trusted name in the aquaculture, poultry, and veterinary health care industry for over 24 years. Founded with a vision to provide high-quality, affordable health care products, we have grown to become a leading manufacturer and exporter serving clients across 50+ countries.

Our commitment to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction has earned us numerous accolades, including the prestigious Government of India Excellence in Export Award. We continue to invest in research and development to bring cutting-edge solutions to our clients worldwide.
